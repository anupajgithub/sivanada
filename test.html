Jennifer Zelvin McCloskey, LL.M, J.D.

General Counsel & Chief Fiduciary Officer

Jennifer is a Founder and General Counsel/Chief Fiduciary Officer of Clermont Trust USA. With over 15 years of experience in business planning, estate planning, and trust administration, she is also licensed to practice law in Delaware, Pennsylvania, Florida, and North Carolina.

In 2017, Jennifer developed a minor course of study at the University of Delaware where she serves as the Director of the Lerner College Trust Management Minor. She also has taught Business Law, Fiduciary Income Tax, Trust Management Symposium, and Trusts/Other Fiduciary Entities. Additionally, Jennifer administered Delaware trust instruments for ultra-high and high net worth individuals at J.P. Morgan Trust Company of Delaware.

Jennifer received her Master of Laws in Taxation from Villanova University, Charles Widger School of Law, her Juris Doctorate from Delaware Law School, cum laude, and her bachelor’s degree from Florida State University. She also served as an Assistant State Attorney in the 19th Judicial Circuit of Florida.

Amanda Luu, CTFA

Trust Officer

Amanda Luu is a Trust Officer at Clermont Trust USA, working directly with HNW and UHNW families and their advisors to deliver exceptional fiduciary and trust management services. With a background in banking and trust administration, she brings both technical knowledge and a client-centered approach to her role.

Amanda strives to provide thoughtful solutions tailored to each client’s unique circumstances. Her professional philosophy is based on building long-term, generational relationships rooted in trust and transparency. She is dedicated to protecting family legacy and guiding clients through the complexities of fiduciary services with clarity and care.

Amanda holds a Master’s degree in Professional Accountancy and a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration and Economics from Augustana University. She has also earned the Certified Trust and Fiduciary Advisor (CTFA) designation, underscoring her commitment to excellence in the wealth management field. Outside of work, Amanda enjoys traveling to new places and spending time with family and friends.

Bill Hogan, CPA

Board Advisor and Interim Chief Financial Officer

Bill serves as a board observer and advisor, as well as the interim Chief Financial Officer of Clermont Trust USA.

Bill spent the first 12 years of his career at Deloitte after graduating from the State University of New York at Geneseo. He moved from the public accounting field to serve in the finance role leading several multinational organizations. He has significant experience in both public and private equity owned businesses and has held the Executive Vice President and Chief Financial Officer at several companies owned by major private equity sponsors resulting in successful liquidity events at all of these investments.

Bill has served as an officer and director of several professional organizations and brings extensive experience in mergers and acquisitions, strategic transactions, corporate development, public and private financings, as well as Sarbanes-Oxley and PCAOB regulatory compliance.

Bill and his wife, Lydia, met at SUNY Geneseo and have been happily married for over 35 years. They currently reside on Long Island, New York.
